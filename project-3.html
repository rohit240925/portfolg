Smart Tank Management System

A Smart Tank Management System is an Internet of Things (IoT) based solution designed to monitor and manage water storage tanks in urban, society, and municipal settings. The main problem that this system aims to solve is the inefficient and manual process of monitoring water levels in tanks, leading to water shortages, overflow, and mismanagement. Project Overview

smart tank management system is a project that involves the use of sensors, microcontrollers, and network communication technologies to monitor and control the level of liquid in a tank remotely. The system can be used for various applications, such as in water tanks, fuel tanks, and chemical storage tanks. The sensors are used to measure the level of the liquid in the tank, and the microcontroller processes the sensor data and sends it to a central control system through a wireless network. The central control system can then be used to monitor the level of the liquid in the tank, detect leaks, and even control the flow of liquid in and out of the tank. The system can also be set up to send notifications and alerts in case of any abnormalities, such as a low tank level or a leak. Overall, a smart tank management system helps to optimize tank usage, reduce waste, and enhance safety and reliability.
